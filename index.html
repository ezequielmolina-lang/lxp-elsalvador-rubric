What you're looking at

This is the complete rubric the World Bank team used to evaluate three LXP platforms proposed for national deployment in El Salvador in 2026: IHFB (Korean origin, donated by Korean cooperation), Kira Learning (Andrew Ng + Anthropic), and Grok (xAI, under negotiation with the government). Each of the 98 cells was evaluated with PASS / PARTIAL / FAIL / N/A criteria backed by traceable evidence. For the full findings report, see the Assessment PDF.
